Types of eagles have been met in Finland:

  • Golden eagle (Aquila chrysaetos)
  • White-tailed eagle (Haliaeetus albicilla)
  • Steppe eagle (Aquila nipalensis)
  • Imperial eagle (Aquila heliaca)
